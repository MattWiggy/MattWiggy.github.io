Why ski in Montana?

Everyone knows Montana is known for its cold and snowy winters. Although it gets very cold, there are plenty of activities to do all winter long. One of the most popular winter activities is skiing. Skiing is popular at resorts like Showdown Montana, Teton Pass, Discovery Ski Area, Maverick Mountain, and Whitefish Mountain Resort. These are my personal favorite in Montana for a variety of reasons. Many of these places get huge dumps of snow many times a year that bring in skiers from states all around. Even if skiing isn't your thing there are still plenty of activities. Many of these resorts are close to town, and have plenty of activities to do after a ski day, to calm down and relax. For example, many people like come warm up in a natural hot tub at Elkhorn Hotsprings. This is one of the many hotsprings in Montana that are cheap and easily accessable for everyone, and not to mention, only minutes away from a ski resort.
